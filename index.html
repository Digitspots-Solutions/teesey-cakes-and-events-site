Your Trusted Partner for Cakes and Celebrations

Teesey Cakes and Events is a premium bakery and event planning service located in Alimosho, Lagos. We specialize in creating custom cakes that are not just desserts, but edible works of art that capture the essence of your special moments.

Whether you're planning a wedding, birthday, corporate event, or any celebration, our experienced team combines culinary expertise with creative event planning to deliver experiences that exceed expectations. From the first consultation to the final detail, we're committed to making your event truly memorable.

Our state-of-the-art facility at Pipeline Road, Aboru ensures every cake is baked fresh using premium ingredients, while our event planning services handle everything from décor to coordination, allowing you to simply enjoy your special day.

What We Offer

Custom Cakes

Wedding cakes, birthday cakes, anniversary cakes, and celebration cakes designed specifically for your event. Choose from our signature flavors or request something unique. Every cake is handcrafted with premium ingredients and decorated to perfection.

Delivery Services

Safe, timely delivery across Lagos. We ensure your cake arrives in perfect condition, ready to wow your guests.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Our Services

How far in advance should I order my cake?

For custom celebration cakes, we recommend ordering at least 2 weeks in advance. For wedding cakes, please contact us 4-6 weeks before your date. Rush orders may be accommodated depending on our schedule.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes! We deliver across Lagos, with standard delivery to most areas within Alimosho LGA. Delivery fees vary based on distance and cake size. We ensure your cake arrives safely and on time.

Can I customize my cake flavor and design?

Absolutely! We work closely with you to create exactly what you envision. Choose from our popular flavors including chocolate, vanilla, red velvet, fruit cake, and more. Share your design ideas, photos, or theme and we'll bring it to life.

What are your payment terms?

We require 50% deposit to confirm your order, with the balance due upon delivery or pickup. We accept cash, bank transfer, and mobile payments.

Do you cater to dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer eggless cakes and can accommodate certain dietary needs. Please inform us of any restrictions when placing your order.

What areas do you serve for event planning?

While based in Alimosho, we provide event planning services across Lagos State. Contact us to discuss your event location and requirements.
